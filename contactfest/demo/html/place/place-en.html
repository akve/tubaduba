Venue


This time we said NO to cities and long transitions between studios.. and went looking for an amiable place to dance in the nature. Where we could be together from the beginning till the end. To spend more time together and to do more dancing..

We found a wonderful place in the heart of history of Ukraine, near the bank of Dnipro river, the Ganga of of the Ukraine. There is a also a comfortable sport camp with welcoming hosts. The center has several incredible spaces for long lasting, spacious and meditative jams.

Coordinates: 50°01'40.6"N 31°26'22.2"E or 50.027944, 31.439500

Accommodation


We'll stay in cozy rooms, 2-4 people per room, with all the necessary facilities. You can also take your child if you want, see 'conditions' section for details.


Food


We will have delicious vegetarian traditional food (adapted for light and continuous dancing) three times a day.


Space for kids


Besides all the infrastructure of a "recreational sport camp for kids", where we stay, we will also have a separate comfortable play-room for kids. During morning and afternoon classes all kids age of 4 and older will be able to stay there under supervision of one of the festival volunteers. Kids under age 4 will be able to stay there under a parent’s supervision.

We will try to make sure there are enough toys and art materials. We will be also greatful if you can bring some interesting items for your kids.
