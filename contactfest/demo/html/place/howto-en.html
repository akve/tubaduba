Via Poland


It's really easy and much cheaper to get to Ukraine from Poland.
When searching for a ticket, try to find one to Poland on www.azair.eu
Traveling to Kiev (the nearest city to the festival) from Poland will cost around 30 euros. To check bus tickets ask your russian/ukrainian speaking friend to check it here busfor.ua or write us.


From Kiev


The spot can be reached by minibus departing from bus stations in Kiev, from metro stations Kharkivska and Chernigivska. Ask for a bus to "Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky", and exit there on the terminus, bus station. The bus takes around 1.5 hours to get from Kiev to Pereyaslav. Then ask for a taxi to "Slavutich" camp. This is 10 mins ride, approximate cost is 50-60 UAH.

We'll probably organize a transfer from Kiev. To book a transfer please send a message to our registration email.
