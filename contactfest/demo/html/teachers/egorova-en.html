Tonya Egorova

Ukraine


Dancer,choreographer

Finished Municipal Higher Educational Institution “Vinnytsia M.D. Leontovych College of Culture and Arts”, Kyiv University of Culture and Arts (Master of Arts Program, Contemporary dance). My teachers are Ruslan Baranov, T.Vinokurova, L.Mova, K. Shishkaryova, O.Kebas, Anna Ozyorskaya, Tomas Mattler, Yasmeem Godder, Manuel Ronda, and others. Was studying modern-jazz dance, contemporary techniques, improvisation and contact improvisation, performance.

Worked in performances by L.Mova, T.Vinokurova, Ruslan Baranov, Rafal Dziemidok , K.Popov, Olive Bieringa, J.Artyomenko, Tamara Maksimenko.

Was teacher of improvisation , performance and contact in Push.Ok , CarpatSKi festivals.

Taking part in contemporary dance and contact improvisation festivals ( War So Vie Theatre, Lublin Dance Theatre, Siberian, Italian, Kyiv, CarpatSKI, Shantambala CI festivals, Nomadic College, Zelyonka Fest, Gogol Fest).

Creating performances for dancers ,Aesthetic Group Gymnastic and sports acrobatics.

Organizated Isadora Dance Group, Contemporary Dance LAB (Human Connections performance), Body-Mind evening of dance performance in Vinnytsia.

Interested in movements in all its forms and manifestations. Researching solo and group improvisation, dance performance. Lаst time was researching movement in martial arts as aikido, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and He chong and work in vіdeo performance.
