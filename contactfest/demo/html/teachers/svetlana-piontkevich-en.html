Svetlana Piontkevich

Ukraine

The class will consist of two parts. In the first part we will explore moving from the center towards the periphery and in reverse, building support and practicing principals of falling. And in the second part we will look into different instruments of improvisation, creativity, and how to make our dance interesting not only for ourselves but also for others.

Bio:

In 2010 Svetlana graduated from the Academy of Management in the Field of Culture and Arts as choreographer and teacher of modern dance. During her studies Svetlana was a member of dance company “Lelio”. She also danced in “Psychedelics” free movement theatre, studied in Oikos contemporary dance school (Kyiv) and later started teaching there.

Since 2010 she started practicing CI and performing with Ruslan Baranov.

Participated in projects directed by Benno Voorham, Thomas Mettler and Ruslan Baranov.

Her teachers include: Frey Faust (Axis Sillabus), Alexander Adriyashkin (contemporary and improvisation), Emmanuelle Gorda (contemporary), Francesca Pedulla (afro), Mariya Udod (contemporary), Eszter Gal (improvisation and CI), Thomas Mettler (improvisation), Benno Voorham (improvisation and CI), Nita Little (improvisation and CI), Brenton Cheng (CI), Andrew Harwood (CI) and others.

Currently teaching CI and participating in various dance projects.
