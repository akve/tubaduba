Viktor Ruban

Ukraine/France

Seeing through


To see wider perspective, to mix and not to be certain, to risk and to review own limitations. Be honest with yourself and what you do - be it a private talk, dancing space, or performing for the audience... That's what I enjoy noticing in myself and in other people. And that's what we'll do on this class.

Bio:

RESEARCH INTERESTS: Strongly inspired by the works of Jaques Ranciere, Roland Barthes, John Dewey and Franko “Bifo” Berardi. Explores the correlations between methods of choreography, poetry and cinematography; the link between individual and political, private and public, theoretical and practical aspects of life. Explores the actual possibilities of mediation between contemporary artists and their audience. Interested in experimenting with other artists in the interdisciplinary projects trying to search for choreographic tools that are appropriated and can be applied to any kind of art. In any interdisciplinary project, eager to challenge the place and position of the artist ́s body, in its artistic process today.
