Inna Falkova

Ukraine

"BODY LANGUAGES"


Class for beginners and advanced (principles day).

We all come from different places, we all have our habits, our different languages. Yet we can communicate through the dance with all our being, our bodies, and with all our qualities and our emotions, despite our differences and our peculiar features. To do this, we have at our disposal a wide repertoire as: technical physical movements, emotions and feelings, life stories, games and humor, philosophical discussions, poetry, and of course some styles of contact-danse, etc ...

What is the specificity repertoire of your body expressions? In this workshop, we are going to look for communication through our own choices, our collective choices and with a particular partner.

What are our methods to express what we are and to find our collective way through the environment around us? We will try to elucidate some pieces of this issue by means of personal movement, contact improvisation and group improvisation.

Bio:

Inna gives workshops of contact improvisation, performance and authentic movement in Ukraine. Besides Inna works at Kharkov University of Arts, where she teaches future actors dance and theatre improvisation techniques. Inna is an organisator of theater and dance perfomances where she participates as well. She has a dance-therapy education which she recieved in Moscow and she uses some of these therapeutic practicies in her teaching.

"My personal research is simplicity, embodiment and expressing life experience in movement, dance as language, as international language".

Inna has been teaching dance and stage movement in the university of Arts since 1997.

Inna has been dancing contact improvisation since 2000, teaching contact improvisation and performance since 2005, gave workshops on CI festivals and training in Ukraine, in Georgia, in Moldavia, in Russia, in France, in Switzerland.
