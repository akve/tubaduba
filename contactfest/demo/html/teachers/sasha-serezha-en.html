The depth comes from the ease


The dance of CI is a great tool for body dialog with a partner, with space, with yourself.
CI consists of "physics" of our body, "chemistry" of the states, emotions, rhythms, breathing.

Sure thing, we often use something from our experience and lessons learned by the body. This makes us feel more confident, more skilled. But when basing only on this, we get rid of something new and amusing.
We'll try, without overcomplications, go with the forms of our personal language. Not exaggerating, but accepting as it is, accepting and transforming your state into physical potential in the current moment.
We allow to have pauses, allow not to hurry up, wait, and follow the curiosity that has no fatigue.
At the same time we'll notice how simple things evolve and the amplitude of our physics grows... This class will be interesting for both novices and "experts" in dance. To dance and to accept, to hear and ... :))
