CI Intensive for kids and grown-ups!
«Learning the world through playing»


How amazing! There will be a time and place to dance all together, kids and parents!
Together with other kids, together with other adults.
Dance! Sing! Share! Create seen and invisible contacts :))
 
WE PROPOSE TO SPEND TIME TOGETHER, IN FREE MOVEMENT, SOUND AND PLAY.

The most valuable (and priceless) - is to feel the kids' trust, mostly not to the parents but to themselves: to their source of art, to their identity, to their possibilities of body, voice, imagination.
The same applies to us, adults.
Everyone of us can be a guide to someone on this playful journey!
Through imagery and games, we'll be travelling to different worlds. We'll be going deep, climb mountains, fly, safely fall, challenge obstacles, build cities, meet unknown creatures, share the experiences of trust, flexibility, courage, curiosity, humor, attention, listening, flow...
Possibly, we'll create new games and new Universes built on our imagination
To support the art space:
- Dance as a play, as a meeting, as communication, as meeting point of two creative sources. 
– Singing. Free sound, rhytmic games 
- Curiosity, trust, play and improvisation

P.S. This space is for kids and parents of any age
