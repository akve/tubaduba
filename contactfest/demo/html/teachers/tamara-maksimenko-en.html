Tamara Maksimenko

Ukraine

My class will be about group interactions, structuring space, listening people who are in the same place. We will take several basic principles of solo and contact improvisation and will try to create something alive on a basis of improvisational structures.

Bio:

Tamara Maksimenko - dancer, performer, and teacher.

She graduated from the school of choreography E.Budnitskoy "Flows" with honors. For a long time was the artist of that theater. 2015 - Training in Physics Institute in Intragna (Switzerland), teacher Thomas Mattler. Currently enrolled by the bodily therapist at the Medical University of Dnepropetrovsk.

Work together with Benno Voorham (Sweden), Ekhard Muller (France), Alexandra Soshnikova and Sergey Golovnya (Moldova), Ruslan Santa (Kiev), with Boroditskiy Denis (Moscow), Christina Shishkareva (Kiev), had experience with Sharlotta Ruth ( Sweden) in a Swedish-Ukrainian project "MOVE" and Alexander Andriyashkin (Russia), Lyudmila Mova, Olga Kebas, Ester Gal, Ekhart Muller. Currently work with Thomas Mattlerom (Switzerland) in projects of physical theater.

She has participated in international festivals Contemporary dance and contact improvisation, as an educator. She is the winner of the "TOTEM dance solo festival" in 2013. Member showcase ZELENKA FEST 2011 2014 2015 Teacher of the Kiev International Festival PUSHOK. Creator of Air Project "NEW LIFE", which was first shown in Ukraine in the summer of 2014. The creator of the project "Femminile" 2015.

The founder of the dance school in Dnepropetrovsk HIGH DANCE COMPANY.
