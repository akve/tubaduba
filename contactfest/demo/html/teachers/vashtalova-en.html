Ekaterina Vashtalova

Ukraine

Catherine Vashtalova - the founder and trainer of "Embodied Freedom" International Project. Bodymind researcher and practitioner.

Certified specialist in Hanna Somatic Education and Clinical Somatics (The Ingle Institute for Somatic Movement Education); certified Sound therapist (Harmonic Sounds: The Association of Sound Therapy).

Studied Feldenkrais Method (J.Wegier, E.Bloom), Craniosacral therapy (The Upledger Institute), Integrative Dance Movement therapy (А.Girshon), Embodiment Integration training (M.Walsh), Axis Syllabus (F.Faust, F.Pedulla), Butoh & Performance (V.Tszin, S.Heller, Т.Kudo), Contact Improvisation (E.Mueller) etc.

Professional interpreter in the field of psychology, somatic therapy and non-verbal communication. The author and coach of "Bodymind English" language courses.
